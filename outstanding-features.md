1. The Art Style: Neon-Vector / Cyberpunk Minimalist
   Since you want to keep the player as a geometric circle with lightning arcs, a Neon-Vector / "Glow-Fi" Cyberpunk style is the most natural and striking evolution.
   The Vibe: High-contrast, sharp geometric lines, and strong emissive (glowing) bloom effects. Think of games like Geometry Wars, Thomas Was Alone, or Cyber Shadow, but with a smoother, vector-based finish.

   Why it works: It allows you to transition away from flat, solid shapes without requiring complex hand-drawn pixel art or detailed 3D models. The "impression" comes entirely from vibrant lighting, fluid animations, and juice.
